FAQ

  • How do I suggest a book?

    Go to the Contact Us page, select 'Add a Book' as the Category, and write down your suggested book in the message box.

  • I am unable to login. What do I do?

    Check if you have entered your password and email-ID correctly. If everything seems to be correct, send us a message via the Contact Us page under Technical Support.

  • I would like to report a bug/I am facing some issues while using the website.

    Send us a message under the category of Technical Support.

  • Can I delete my account?

    While there is no feature to delete your account, if you send us a message, we can remove it from our side.

  • I have some feedback/suggestions to give regarding the website. Who do I contact?

    We would love to hear what you have to say! Hop on over to the Contact Us page and send us the message!
